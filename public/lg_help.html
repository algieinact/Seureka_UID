Frequently Ask Question (FAQ)

To create a new community on Seureka, go to the "Community" section and click on the "Create Community" button. Fill in the required details and submit the form.

Yes, you can join multiple communities and participate in various discussions. Simply navigate to the community or discussion you are interested in and click "Join".

To filter discussions by category, go to the "Discussions" section and use the filter options available on the sidebar. Select the categories you are interested in to view relevant discussions.

To report inappropriate content, click on the "Report" button located next to the content. Provide a brief explanation of why you are reporting the content and submit the report.

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.
